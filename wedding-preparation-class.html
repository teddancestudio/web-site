Wedding Preparation Class

We teach and choreograph the First Dance to the music of your choice (if you don't have a song, we help you find music from our wide collection). Our skilled instructors will teach you how to walk on the dance floor, look confident and relaxed, and finish your First Dance in a romantic pose. So get one less worry off your head and start learning your First Dance now!

Our Studio is a very busy place, so in order to avoid disappointment of not being able to book your lessons at your preferred times - please book as early as possible.

Cost: $40.00 for 55 min, $20.00 for 30 min (tax included).

Group rate is also available for up to 4 couples from your Wedding Party.
