Private Class

Private classes are perfect for learning and polishing your steps or technique while getting an undivided attention from your teacher. You'll learn only the dances that interest you, at the pace that is comfortable for you.

Private lessons are subject to teacher and floor availability and must be booked in advance.

Cost: $40.00 - $45.00 for 55 min. lesson.
